### 🧠 About Me

MS in Computer & Information Systems @ **Northeastern University, Boston**

I'm a Data Scientist who doesn't stop at building models — I automate the entire analytical workflow. From designing multi-agent AI systems that detect pricing anomalies across millions of records, to training fraud detection models on insurance claims data, I focus on turning messy business problems into automated, production-ready solutions.

- 🤖 Designed **agentic AI systems** with **CrewAI & LangChain** to automate pricing anomaly detection across **10,000+ stores**
- 🔍 Built NLP pipelines (**BERT, Qwen LLM, spaCy**) supporting **~$2M in audit recovery** at PRGX Global
- 📊 Automated fraud detection on **500K+ insurance records**, identifying **18% more fraudulent claims**
- ⚙️ Built end-to-end **automated data analysis pipelines** — from EDA to model training to stakeholder dashboards
